ABOUT

The iPhone was a revolutionary phone made by Apple, the first one released in 2007. Development for the phone began in 2004 when Apple gathered 1000 employees to work on a confidential product. The user interface was built around the device’s multi-touch screen and a virtual keyboard. As of 2019, Apple has released twelve generations of iPhones. To scroll, a user simply needed to swipe up or down to scroll, as if you are physically moving the page. Additionally, when a page reaches to the end, the screen will “bounce”. This is known as elastic scrolling.
