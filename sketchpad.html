Sutherland's Sketchpad
1963
CREATOR
Ivan Sutherland
ABOUT

As one of the first graphical user interfaces, Sketchpad was a revolutionary program that was written by Ivan Sutherland in 1963. It was one of the first computer aided program and was a major breakthrough in computer graphics development. To scroll, Sketchpad used two knobs, one to scroll vertically and another to scroll horizontally.
