HOW TO USE THE SCROLLBAR

The Mac placed the scrollbar on the right to fit their idea of resizable windows. The users could also move the screen up and down by clicking either above or below the grabbable area of the scroll bar.

ABOUT

The Apple Macintosh (later rebranded as the Machintosh 128K) was one of Apple’s first personal computer released in 1984 and has one of the most well known operating systems today. It was originally sold for $2495 which is equivalent to about $6017 today. It was the first mass-market personal computer that featured a graphical user interface, built-in screen and mouse. The Mac placed the scrollbar on the right to fit their idea of resizable windows. The users could also move the screen up and down by clicking either above or below the grabbable area of the scroll bar.
